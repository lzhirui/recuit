NO.1 Do your research ahead of the interview

It is very important to do some research on the country you plan to work and live in so that you are aware of the different norms and customs. Nothing could be worse than attending a interview without knowing and understan ding the basics of that country’s norms.

NO.2 Frequently asked questions

Tell us about yourself?

To give a brief introduction of yourself, starting with your name, educational background, experience and all the places you have previously worked in. Everything you say should be relevant to the job you are interviewing for.

Why did you quit your previous job?

TNever talk ill of your previous workplace. Instead, talk about how you want to start a new journey and what you have learned from previous work experiences.

How long do you plan to stay in China?

It is important to ensure the interviewer that you plan to stay in China for at least one year. (normal contract length)

Why should we consider hiring you?

Highlight your unique strengths, skills, training and experience. Do research about the job and the company you are applying for, it will really come in handy.
